# Generate Ebook Case Study

Create a case study that proves the ebook's methodology works.

## Case Study Structure

### 1. Subject Profile
- Company / Person name (real or anonymized)
- Industry, size, role
- Starting situation / pain point

### 2. The Challenge
- Specific problem they faced
- What they tried before (and why it failed)
- Stakes / urgency

### 3. The Solution
- How they applied the ebook's methodology
- Specific steps taken
- Timeline

### 4. Implementation Details
- Tools used
- Team involved
- Obstacles overcome

### 5. Results
- Before / after metrics
- Time to results
- Unexpected benefits

### 6. Lessons Learned
- What worked best
- What they'd do differently
- Advice for readers in similar situations

## Output Format

```markdown
## Case Study: [Subject]

### Background
[Profile...]

### The Challenge
[Problem description...]

### The Solution
[How [method from ebook] was applied...]

### Implementation
[Details...]

### Results
| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| [Metric] | X | Y | +Z% |

### Key Takeaways
1. [Lesson]
```
