# Research Topic for Ebook

Given a topic, conduct comprehensive research to support ebook content.

## Research Dimensions

### 1. Landscape Analysis
- What ebooks already exist on this topic?
- What angles are underserved?
- What formats are most successful (length, depth, style)?

### 2. Audience Pain Points
- Common questions from forums, Reddit, StackOverflow
- Misconceptions that need correcting
- What beginners struggle with most
- What advanced users wish they knew earlier

### 3. Key Sources
- Official documentation
- Academic papers (arXiv, Google Scholar)
- Industry reports (Gartner, Forrester)
- Books (Google Books, Amazon reviews for gaps)
- Expert blogs and talks

### 4. Data Points
- Statistics that support the topic
- Trends and growth metrics
- Survey results
- Case study outcomes

```markdown
### Landscape Summary

### Recommended Angles
1. [Fresh perspective]
```
