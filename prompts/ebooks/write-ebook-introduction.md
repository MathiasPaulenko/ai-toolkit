# Write Ebook Introduction

Write an introduction that converts browsers into readers.

## Structure

### 1. The Hook (Opening)
- Start with a story, surprising stat, or relatable frustration
- Make the reader feel seen: "If you've ever struggled with..."
- Keep it under 300 words

### 2. The Promise (Why This Book)
- What specific outcome will the reader achieve?
- Who is this for (and not for)?
- What makes this approach different?

### 3. The Credibility (Why You)
- Brief author bio (relevant experience only)
- Social proof (if applicable)
- Your unique perspective or methodology

### 4. The Map (How to Use This Book)
- Chapter overview (1 sentence each)
- Suggested reading paths (linear vs. topic hopping)
- Prerequisites (if any)

### 5. The Invitation
- Direct address to the reader
- Set expectations for effort and outcomes
- Final motivational push

## Tone Guidelines
- Warm but authoritative
- No humble-bragging
- Acknowledge reader's intelligence
- Promise value, not miracles

## Output

```markdown
# Introduction

[Hook paragraph...]

## What You'll Learn

[Promise section...]

## Who This Is For

[Audience definition...]

## How to Read This Book

[Reading guide...]

## A Note from the Author

[Personal invitation...]
```
