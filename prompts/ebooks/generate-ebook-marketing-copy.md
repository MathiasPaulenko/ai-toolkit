# Generate Ebook Marketing Copy

Generate promotional copy for an ebook across channels.

## Landing Page Copy

### Hero Section
- **Headline**: Benefit-driven, curiosity gap
- **Subheadline**: Expand the promise
- **CTA**: Clear action (Download, Get Free Access)

### Problem Section
- "Does this sound familiar?"
- 3-5 pain point bullets
- Agitate before presenting solution

### Solution Section
- What the ebook covers
- Key takeaways (3-5 bullets)
- Preview of what's inside

### Social Proof
- Testimonials (or template placeholders)
- Download count / social shares
- Author credibility

### FAQ
- 3-5 objections handled

## Email Sequence

### Email 1: Announcement
- Subject line (3 options)
- Short, exciting, CTA to download

### Email 2: Value Teaser
- Share one insight from the ebook
- "This is just one of 10 strategies inside..."

### Email 3: Reminder
- For non-openers
- Social proof + urgency (if applicable)

## Social Media

### Twitter/X (3 variants)
- Hook stat
- Curiosity-driven
- Direct benefit

### LinkedIn (2 variants)
- Professional angle
- Story-driven post
